# Exercise 01: Create knowledge articles


One of the features of the Customer Intentagent is the ability to provide solutions. These solutions can come from knowledge articles, or even from agents. When you configure the rest of the intent agent, you'll be associating some resolutions with specific intents. To ensure the correct solution is being provided, you're going to create a few knowledge articles that you'll attach to some of the intents that you're going to create.

{: .note }
> Under normal circumstances, you'd potentially have multiple articles associated with an intent, but for our purposes, you're going to create a single article to associate with a specific intent.  This will provide you with something repeatable that you can use when showcasing the feature to customers.
