## Task 01 (Optional): Demo self-service in the portal


We want to be able to answer questions related to warranty claims. To do this you'll expose warranty information to a self-service agent. To collect that data, you'll need to first create a warranty table in Dynamics 365 that can store the details.

1. Before you access your portal, open the customer **Service Workspace app** and sign in as a user with the **Customer Service Representative** role.

1. If you do not already have it open in a different tab, open a new browser tab, and navigate to [https://make.powerapps.com](https://make.powerapps.com).

1. Go to **Apps** and locate the **Customer Self-Service Portal** that you created in the previous exercise.

1. Open the **Customer Self Service Portal**.

1. Sign into the portal by using the administrative credentials for your demo environment.

1. Select **Chat**.

1. Wait for the agent to load and tell it that you're **having a problem with your LCD screen**.

1. Wait for the agent to respond.

1. Tell the agent that you want to **Talk to a live person**.

1. Your call will be transferred.
