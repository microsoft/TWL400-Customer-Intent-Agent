# Exercise 05: Demonstrate features to a customer


Now that you've configured the feature to meet our needs, you're going to demo the functionality to the customer. Typically, intent and the intent agent will be part of a larger overarching demo.

## Talk track

Imagine a customer in Brazil has contacted us about their machine which is no longer brewing coffee as intended. After some self-service trouble shooting the case is escalated to a representative who is going to assist the customer in troubleshooting and resolving their issue.

### Business value statements:

These represent themes that should be part of your narrative. As you demo the functionality to your customer you should come back to these themes and reiterate them throughout you demo.

- Improve customer satisfaction with faster, more accurate responses
- Streamline case resolution with intelligent case management and AI assistance

### Items to highlight as you're demoing:

- Emphasize how **Copilot** reduces agent effort with real-time suggestions.
- Highlight how **Customer Intent Agent** Identifies the intent of the customer reaching out.
- Show Representatives can use instructions to assist them in capturing data.
- Demonstrate how solutions can be attached to specific intents to reduce the time it takes to resolve an issue..

{: .warning }
> This demo talk track for the case management agent is part of a bigger overall demonstration. Typically, the Case management Agent would automatically create the case for you, and the Knowledge management agent assist in automatically creating articles.
>
> Each of those two talk tracks will be covered in future labs. **Configure the Case Management agent** and **Configure the Knowledge Management agent**.
