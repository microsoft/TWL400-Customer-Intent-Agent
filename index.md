## Use case / scenario

Contoso Coffee is a multinational manufacturer of gourmet coffee and commercial coffee machines. With over 25,000 employees and offices in 18 countries (including the US, Germany, Japan, Brazil, India, and the UK), their customer service operations are centralized in three global hubs but support multilingual, multi-channel interactions across time zones.

As you did discovery with Contoso, you were able to identify many different challenges as it relates to their call center. There were two significant items that came up. One key challenge was related to the assignment and routing of calls to individuals. Their current Genesys system has very limited capabilities and struggles to capture relevant information to effectively identify the customers issue. This is causing items to be routed to incorrect people, but it is also causing the resolution process to take a lot longer than they believe it should.

Some key points that you were able to pull out were:

- Customers are very dissatisfied as they are often shuffled around multiple times until they are with the correct person.

- Lack of understanding of the customer's intent, who is engaging with us, is causing many different customer frustrations.

- Lack of understanding is also increasing the length of time that it takes for representatives to resolve Issues.

### Business challenges you're trying to solve:

- Reduce average case resolution time and improve first contact resolution.

- Use AI to improve the call routing process by intelligently identifying the intent of the customer who is reaching out to use.
